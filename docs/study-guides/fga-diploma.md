# FGA Diploma Study Guide

Study guide aligned with the Gem-A Diploma in Gemmology curriculum.

## Course Overview

The Diploma course builds on Foundation knowledge, covering advanced topics including origin determination, advanced treatments, grading, and comprehensive gem identification.

## Module 1: Advanced Physical Properties

### Learning Objectives

- Master crystal morphology
- Understand twinning and its significance
- Apply advanced SG techniques

### Key Topics

1. **Crystal Morphology**
   - Forms and habits
   - Miller indices
   - Crystal growth

2. **Twinning**
   - Contact twins
   - Penetration twins
   - Polysynthetic twinning

3. **Advanced SG**
   - Accuracy considerations
   - Composite stones
   - Unusual materials
